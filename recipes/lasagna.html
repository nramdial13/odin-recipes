Lasagna Recipe

Simple and quick Lasagna recipe which will feed 6!

This recipe can be modified to be cook 4 or 6 servings in less than 90 minutes.

Ingredients is as followed:

  • 2 Eggs
  • Lasagna noodles pre-cooked
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps:

  1. Boil Tomato Sauce for 20 minutes until water content decresed
  2. Mix eggs with other filling items
  3. Lay one layer of noodles at the bottom of dish and cover with tomato sauce and filling, repeat sequence of layering again and finish with one top layer of noodles
